Detailed Problem Statement

A housing society wants to manage water tank levels, daily water usage, tanker supply, pump activity, and apartment-wise consumption digitally using SQL.

The society has multiple water tanks installed in different towers. Sensors record water levels at regular intervals. When water levels become low, water tankers are ordered. The system also tracks pump usage, apartment consumption, tanker delivery, and payment status.

The system should store:

Tower details
Water tank details
Sensor reading details
Apartment details
Apartment water usage
Tanker supplier details
Water tanker delivery details
Pump activity details

The system should help the society admin answer questions like:

Which tanks have low water level?
Which tower consumes the most water?
Which tanker deliveries are pending?
Which supplier delivered the most water?
How much tanker payment is pending?
What is the complete water usage report?
