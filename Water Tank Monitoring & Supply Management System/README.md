# 🚰 Water Tank Monitoring & Supply Management System

## 📌 Overview
A system to monitor water tank levels, supply schedules, and water distribution operations.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track water tank levels
- Monitor water supply schedules
- Prevent water shortages
- Improve supply management

## 🧱 System Components
- Water Tanks
- Supply Vehicles
- Distribution Records
- Maintenance Logs

## 🗂️ Database Design
- water_tanks
- supply_vehicles
- distribution_records
- maintenance_logs

## ⚙️ Key Features
- Water level monitoring
- Supply schedule tracking
- Distribution analytics
- Maintenance management

## 📊 Sample Queries
- Low water level alerts
- Supply distribution trends
- Tank maintenance reports
- Water consumption analysis

## 💡 Use Cases
- Municipal water departments
- Smart water systems
- Rural water supply projects

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
