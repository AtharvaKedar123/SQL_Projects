Problem 46: Electric Vehicle Charging Station Management System
Detailed Problem Statement

An electric vehicle charging company wants to manage its EV charging station network digitally using SQL.

The company has multiple EV users, electric vehicles, charging stations, charger points, charging sessions, power usage records, payments, and charger availability details. Users can register their electric vehicles and charge them at different stations. Each station has multiple charger points with different charger types and charging rates.

The system should also track charging start time, end time, units consumed, total cost, payment status, and whether a charger is available, occupied, under maintenance, or inactive.

The system should store:

EV user details
Vehicle details
Charging station details
Charger point details
Charging session details
Power usage details
Payment details
Charger availability status

The system should help the company answer questions like:

Which charging stations generated the most revenue?
Which users charged their vehicles the most?
Which charger points are currently available?
Which charging sessions are active or completed?
How much total power was consumed?
Which vehicles consumed the most electricity?
Which payments are pending?
