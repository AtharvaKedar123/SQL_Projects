Detailed Problem Statement

A disaster management organization wants to manage its relief camp and resource distribution system digitally using SQL.

The organization has multiple relief camps, victims, volunteers, food packets, medical supplies, donation stock, resource distribution records, and camp-wise inventory details. Victims are registered under different relief camps. Volunteers are assigned to camps and help distribute food, water, medicines, blankets, and other emergency resources.

The system should also track available stock, donated resources, distributed resources, low-stock items, victim needs, volunteer assignments, and camp-wise distribution status.

The system should store:

Relief camp details
Victim details
Volunteer details
Resource category details
Donation stock details
Camp inventory details
Distribution record details
Medical supply details
Food packet details
Low-stock alert details

The system should help the organization answer questions like:

Which camps have low resource stock?
Which victims received resources?
Which volunteers distributed the most items?
Which camp has the highest number of victims?
How much food and medical stock is available?
Which resources were distributed the most?
Which donation items are still available?
