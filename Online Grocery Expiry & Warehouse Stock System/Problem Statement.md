Problem 47: Online Grocery Expiry & Warehouse Stock System
Detailed Problem Statement

An online grocery company wants to manage its grocery inventory, expiry tracking, and warehouse stock system digitally using SQL.

The platform has multiple products, suppliers, warehouses, stock batches, expiry dates, customer orders, ordered items, and stock alert records. Grocery products are supplied by different suppliers and stored in different warehouses. Each product can have multiple stock batches with different manufacturing dates, expiry dates, quantity, and batch status.

The system should also track low-stock products, expired batches, near-expiry batches, customer order status, warehouse-wise stock, and supplier-wise product supply.

The system should store:

Product details
Supplier details
Warehouse details
Stock batch details
Expiry date details
Customer details
Customer order details
Ordered item details
Low-stock alert details
Batch status

The system should help the company answer questions like:

Which products are low in stock?
Which stock batches are expired?
Which products are near expiry?
Which warehouse has the highest stock?
Which suppliers provide the most products?
Which customer orders are pending or delivered?
How much stock is available for each product?
