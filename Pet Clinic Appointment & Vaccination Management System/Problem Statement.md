Problem 25: Pet Clinic Appointment & Vaccination Management System
Detailed Problem Statement

A pet clinic wants to manage pet owners, pets, doctor appointments, vaccination records, treatments, and billing digitally using SQL.

Pet owners can book appointments for checkups, vaccination, grooming, surgery consultation, or emergency care. Each pet has details like species, breed, age, and medical history. The clinic should track appointment status, assigned veterinarian, vaccination due dates, treatment records, and payment status.

The system should store:

Pet owner details
Pet details
Veterinarian details
Appointment details
Vaccination records
Treatment records
Billing and payment details

The system should help the clinic answer questions like:

Which appointments are upcoming?
Which pets have pending vaccinations?
Which veterinarian handled the most appointments?
Which owners have pending payments?
How much revenue was generated?
What is the complete pet medical history?
