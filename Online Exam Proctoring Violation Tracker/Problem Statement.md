Problem 14: Online Exam Proctoring Violation Tracker
Detailed Problem Statement

An online examination platform wants to track student behavior during online exams using SQL.

During an online exam, students may trigger different types of proctoring violations such as tab switching, face not detected, multiple faces detected, microphone noise, mobile phone detected, copy-paste attempt, or suspicious eye movement. Each violation is recorded with time, severity level, and action taken.

The system should store:

Student details
Exam details
Exam session details
Violation type details
Violation logs
Final proctoring result
Risk score

The system should help the exam team answer questions like:

Which students had the highest risk score?
Which violations occurred the most?
Which exam sessions were flagged?
Which students had no violations?
How many high-severity violations occurred?
Which exams had the most suspicious activity?
