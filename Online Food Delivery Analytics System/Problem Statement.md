Problem 2: Online Food Delivery Analytics System
Detailed Problem Statement

An online food delivery company wants to manage its food ordering system digitally using SQL.

The platform has multiple customers, restaurants, menu items, delivery partners, food orders, and payments. Customers can order food from different restaurants. Each restaurant has its own menu items with prices. Once an order is placed, a delivery partner is assigned to deliver the order. The system should also track order status, payment status, and total revenue.

The system should store:

Customer details
Restaurant details
Menu item details
Delivery partner details
Food order details
Ordered item details
Payment details
Delivery status

The system should help the company answer questions like:

Which restaurants generated the most revenue?
Which customers ordered the most?
Which food items are most popular?
Which orders are pending or delivered?
How much total revenue was generated?
Which delivery partner completed the most deliveries?
