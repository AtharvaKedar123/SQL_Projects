Problem 50: Railway Coach Cleaning & Maintenance Tracking System
Detailed Problem Statement

A railway department wants to manage its coach cleaning and maintenance tracking system digitally using SQL.

The department has multiple trains, coaches, cleaning staff, maintenance staff, cleaning schedules, maintenance issues, inspection records, repair details, and repair costs. Each train has multiple coaches. Coaches need regular cleaning before and after trips. Some coaches may also have maintenance issues such as broken seats, AC failure, damaged doors, electrical faults, or water leakage.

The system should also track cleaning status, inspection status, maintenance priority, repair status, assigned staff, and total repair cost.

The system should store:

Train details
Coach details
Cleaning staff details
Maintenance staff details
Cleaning schedule details
Maintenance issue details
Inspection record details
Repair detail records
Cleaning status
Repair cost details

The system should help the railway department answer questions like:

Which coaches are pending cleaning?
Which coaches have maintenance issues?
Which trains have the highest repair cost?
Which staff completed the most cleaning tasks?
Which maintenance issues are still open?
Which coaches passed inspection?
How much total repair cost was generated?
