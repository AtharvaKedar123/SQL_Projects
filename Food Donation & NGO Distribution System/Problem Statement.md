Detailed Problem Statement

A food donation platform wants to manage donors, donated food items, NGOs, beneficiaries, food safety status, pickup requests, and distribution records using SQL.

Restaurants, event halls, supermarkets, and individuals donate extra food. NGOs collect this food, verify its safety, and distribute it to beneficiaries. The platform wants to track food donations, donor activity, NGO assignments, food expiry, safety checks, and distribution performance.

The system should store:

Donor details
NGO details
Beneficiary details
Food donation details
Food safety status
Pickup request details
Distribution records
Donation quantity details
Food expiry details

The system should help the platform answer questions like:

Which donors donated the most food?
Which NGOs distributed the most food?
Which food items are unsafe or expired?
Which pickup requests are pending?
How many beneficiaries received food?
What is the total quantity of food donated?
Which donations are still not distributed?
Which food category is donated the most?
