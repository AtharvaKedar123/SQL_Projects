37. Solar Panel Energy Generation Monitoring System
Detailed Problem Statement

A solar energy company wants to manage solar panels, inverters, energy generation records, faults, maintenance tasks, and daily power output using SQL.

Solar panels are installed at different sites and connected to inverters. The company wants to track how much energy each panel generates daily, detect faulty panels, monitor inverter performance, schedule maintenance, and calculate total power generation.

The system should store:

Solar site details
Solar panel details
Inverter details
Daily energy generation records
Fault details
Maintenance schedule details
Technician details
Panel working status

The system should help the company answer questions like:

Which solar panel generated the most energy?
Which site produced the highest total power?
Which panels are faulty?
Which inverter handled the highest output?
What is the total energy generated?
Which technicians handled the most maintenance tasks?
Which faults are still unresolved?
What is the average daily generation per panel?
