Problem 27: Smart Hotel Housekeeping & Room Service Management System
Detailed Problem Statement

A hotel wants to manage room cleaning, housekeeping staff, guest requests, room service orders, and maintenance issues digitally using SQL.

Guests stay in different rooms and may request cleaning, towels, food service, laundry pickup, or maintenance support. Housekeeping staff are assigned to rooms based on floor and availability. The system should track room status, cleaning status, request priority, assigned staff, completion time, and service billing.

The system should store:

Guest details
Hotel room details
Housekeeping staff details
Guest service request details
Room cleaning schedule
Room service order details
Billing details

The system should help the hotel answer questions like:

Which rooms need cleaning?
Which guest requests are pending?
Which staff handled the most tasks?
Which rooms are currently occupied?
Which service generated the most revenue?
What is the complete guest service report?
