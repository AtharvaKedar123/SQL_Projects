# 🚗 Vehicle Service Center Management System

## 📌 Overview
A system to manage vehicle servicing, customer appointments, repair records, and billing operations.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track vehicle service history
- Manage repair jobs
- Schedule service appointments
- Monitor spare parts inventory

## 🧱 System Components
- Customers
- Vehicles
- Service Records
- Mechanics

## 🗂️ Database Design
- customers
- vehicles
- service_records
- mechanics

## ⚙️ Key Features
- Service appointment booking
- Repair tracking
- Spare parts monitoring
- Billing management

## 📊 Sample Queries
- Most serviced vehicle models
- Mechanic performance reports
- Pending service jobs
- Revenue analysis

## 💡 Use Cases
- Vehicle workshops
- Automobile service centers
- Car maintenance businesses

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- SQL

## 🚀 Future Enhancements
- Online service booking
- Automated service reminders
- AI maintenance prediction
