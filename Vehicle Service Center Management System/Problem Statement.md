Problem 15: Vehicle Service Center Management System
Detailed Problem Statement

A vehicle service center wants to manage customers, vehicles, service bookings, mechanics, spare parts, and billing digitally using SQL.

Customers bring vehicles for different services such as oil change, engine repair, tyre replacement, brake service, washing, or full inspection. Each service job is assigned to a mechanic. If spare parts are used, their quantity and cost must be tracked. The system also records service status and final bill payment.

The system should store:

Customer details
Vehicle details
Mechanic details
Service booking details
Service job details
Spare part details
Spare parts used in service
Billing and payment status

The system should help the service center answer questions like:

Which vehicles are currently under service?
Which mechanic handled the most service jobs?
Which spare parts are used the most?
Which customers have pending payments?
How much revenue was generated?
Which service type is most common?
