39. Smart City Streetlight Fault Monitoring System
Detailed Problem Statement

A smart city authority wants to manage streetlights, sensor readings, power usage, fault detection, technicians, and repair status using SQL.

Streetlights are installed across different city zones and are equipped with sensors that monitor light intensity, power consumption, and operational health. Faults such as bulb failure, power issues, or wiring problems are automatically detected and logged. Technicians are assigned to resolve these issues.

The system should store:

Streetlight details
City zone details
Sensor reading records
Power consumption details
Fault logs
Technician details
Repair and maintenance records
Streetlight operational status

The system should help the city authority answer questions like:

Which streetlights are faulty?
Which zones have the highest number of faults?
Which technician handled the most repairs?
What is the total power consumption?
Which faults are still unresolved?
Which streetlights consume the most power?
What is the average power usage per zone?
Which streetlights are currently inactive?
