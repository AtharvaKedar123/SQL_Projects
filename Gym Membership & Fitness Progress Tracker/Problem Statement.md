Problem 7: Gym Membership & Fitness Progress Tracker
Detailed Problem Statement

A gym wants to manage its members, trainers, membership plans, workout sessions, attendance, and fitness progress digitally using SQL.

Members can join different membership plans such as monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly plans. Each member may be assigned a trainer based on their fitness goal. Members attend workout sessions, and their body progress is recorded over time.

The system should store:

Member details
Trainer details
Membership plan details
Member subscription details
Workout session details
Attendance details
Fitness progress records

The system should help the gym owner answer questions like:

Which members have active memberships?
Which trainers handle the most members?
Which members attend regularly?
Which members improved their weight or body fat percentage?
How much membership revenue was generated?
Which workout type is most popular?
