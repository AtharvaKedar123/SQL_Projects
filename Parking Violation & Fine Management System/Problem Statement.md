Problem 28: Smart Parking Violation & Fine Management System
Detailed Problem Statement

A smart city traffic department wants to manage illegal parking violations, vehicle details, parking zones, fine generation, and payment tracking digitally using SQL.

Vehicles may be parked in no-parking zones, expired parking slots, emergency lanes, reserved areas, or unpaid parking spaces. The system should track violation type, officer details, fine amount, payment status, and repeat offenders.

The system should store:

Vehicle owner details
Vehicle details
Parking zone details
Traffic officer details
Violation type details
Parking violation records
Fine payment details

The system should help the traffic department answer questions like:

Which fines are unpaid?
Which vehicles have repeated violations?
Which parking zone has the most violations?
Which violation type occurs most often?
How much fine revenue was collected?
Which officers reported the most violations?
